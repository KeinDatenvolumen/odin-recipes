Spaghtti Carbonara Recipe

Description

This recipe will allow you to make the best spaghtti carbonrara you have ever tested, the recipe is even used in some high quality restaurants

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1/2 pound pancetta, diced
  • 1 to 2 garlic cloves
  • 3 to 4 whole eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan
  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • Kosher salt and black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat the pasta water
  2. Saute the pancetta and garlic
  3. Beat the eggs and half of the cheese
  4. Cook the pasta
  5. Toss the pasta with pancetta
  6. Add the beaten egg mixture
